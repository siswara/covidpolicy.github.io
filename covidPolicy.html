Covid-19 Related Public Health Policy

In the past couple of months, how does US policy response to covid shapes infection rate. How does this compare to other mathematical modelling and other country.

EARLY PROJECTIONS
In the early months of corona virus pandemic (End of February to March 2020), we can see how projection predicts high rate of increase in number of future covid-19 cases.

Rc represent dampening factor caused by infection prevention strategies (e.g mask usage). High percentage of Rc indicating effectiveness of prevention starategy.

IMPLEMENTATION PUBLIC RESTRICTION
Goverments around the world start implementing policy of public gratheric restriction and/on closure in anticipation of corona pandemic.

EFFECT OF PUBLIC RESTRICTION
As pandemic progresses, we can see the comparison of infection rate prediction against number of recorded cases.

SELF-QUARANTINE EASING
As pandemic progresses, some goverment shows decreases in infection rate and start to relax public closure policy. However this policy affect vary across countries.

FUTURE PROJECTION
With new policy implementation of mandatory mask, social distancing and public reopen will infection rate follow the predictive model? Only time will tell, but we can play our role slowing down the spread by waring mask and social distance.
